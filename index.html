Essence of Kriya Yoga

Kriya Yoga is a living meditation tradition grounded in breath, energy, and conscious attention. It is not a goal-driven system but a way to more intimate relationship with your own presence.

Slow practice, repeated consistently, opens clarity. Every step is an invitation, not an achievement.

How we meet

  • Weekly guided circle – gentle breath, movement, and silent period (90 minutes).
  • Recorded sessions – home practice you can return to in your own rhythm.
  • Retreat days – sustained stillness, group support, resilient quiet.
